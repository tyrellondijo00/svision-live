Our Background


Smart Vision was established in response to an overwhelming need among humanitarian and development organizations for efficient and accurate monitoring, evaluation, and research services. We are driven by a passion to help our clients reach their maximum potential in Somalia and the Horn of Africa. Since our inception 10 years ago, Smart Vision has become an industry leader in Somalia and Somaliland. We have gained an enviable reputation by providing top-rated M&E and research services and have expanded our support to include organizational capacity building.
